About us


To allow job finders to get jobs in startup , be it low level business or a small scale start up looking for a team so that they can build upon, our platform helps in building up a connection between the startups and the people in need of jobs desperately. Our platform will help in networking and will solve a problem for the start ups who are just building out by getting them their desired team so that they can kick start right away.
